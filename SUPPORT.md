# Support — CoveLab Foundation

How support works, in plain terms, with honest expectations. Read this before opening an issue or emailing.

---

## Channels (in order of preference)

1. **Read what's included first.** CoveLab Foundation is source-available — buyers can read the full method guide, the decisions, and the documented pitfall database that ship with the product. Most "how do I…" questions are answered there.

2. **GitHub Issues** — use the templates (bug / question / feature). Public discussion helps the next person too.

3. **Email** — `support@covelab.tech` for license/refund matters or security disclosures. **No SLA.** A solo operator runs CoveLab; expect days, not hours.

---

## What support does NOT cover

- Custom setup on your own server (we can't log into your machine).
- Advice about your specific business or codebase (that's your project; the stack is the tool).
- Guaranteed fixes by a deadline (see targets below — there is no SLA).
- Phone or video calls.

---

## Bug severity (targets, not guarantees)

| Severity | Meaning | Target |
|----------|---------|--------|
| **Critical** | Data loss, or the system won't run at all | ~2 weeks, best-effort |
| **High** | A component misbehaves but a workaround exists | ~4 weeks, best-effort |
| **Medium** | Documentation gap or minor inconsistency | When time allows |
| **Low** | Cosmetic / typo | Pull requests welcome |

Operated by one person with a full-time job. Realistic expectations protect both of us.

---

## Refunds

**30-day, no-questions-asked**, from the date of purchase. Email `support@covelab.tech` with your order ID. Refunds are processed by Polar (the Merchant of Record for your purchase). Polar may also issue refunds within its own window to prevent chargebacks.

---

## Self-fix first

The product is source-available on purpose. If something breaks: read the error, search the included pitfall database, read the relevant decision, inspect the code (no obfuscation), patch locally — then open an issue if the fix belongs upstream. Often faster than waiting for a reply.

---

## Security

Found a security issue? **Do not** open a public issue. Email `support@covelab.tech` with subject `[SECURITY]` and we'll coordinate privately.
